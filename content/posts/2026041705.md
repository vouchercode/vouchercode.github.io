## Why Do Hidden Costs Matter More Than Ever in 2026?

In 2026, with regulatory frameworks tightening globally and market volatility becoming the new normal, every basis point saved on transaction costs directly impacts your net returns. "Hidden costs" refer to all fees and charges not immediately apparent in the advertised trading fee. These include, but are not limited to:

*   **Withdrawal Fees:** The cost to move your assets off an exchange, which can vary dramatically between networks and tokens.
*   **Deposit Fees:** Some networks charge fees for depositing funds, which the user often bears.
*   **Spread Slippage:** The difference between the expected price of a trade and the price at which it is actually executed, often worse during high volatility.
*   **Invitation Code Benefit Degradation:** Some fee discounts or rebates may have caps, expire, or only apply to specific trading pairs.
*   **Currency Conversion Fees:** Costs incurred when funding your account with fiat or converting between stablecoins.

Our test simulated a user depositing $5,000, executing 50 spot and 10 futures trades of varying sizes over three months, and finally withdrawing 70% of the portfolio. The total cost was calculated, and the results focused on the net impact after applying the benefits from each invitation code.
