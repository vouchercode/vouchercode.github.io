## Common Questions Answered (FAQ)

**Q: Is my Secret Recovery Phrase stored by Bitget Wallet?**
A: Absolutely not. Your Secret Recovery Phrase is generated and encrypted locally on your device. Bitget Wallet cannot access or recover it. You are the sole custodian.

**Q: Can I use the same wallet address on different blockchains?**
A: It depends. Some EVM-compatible chains (like Ethereum, Polygon, BSC) may share the same address format. However, non-EVM chains (like Solana, Bitcoin) will have completely different addresses. Always use the "Receive" function for the specific chain you are using.

**Q: What happens if I send tokens to the wrong blockchain network?**
A: Funds sent to an address on an incompatible network are likely lost permanently. This is why double-checking the **network/chain selection** before every transaction is one of the most important safety practices.

**Q: Are the swap rates inside the wallet competitive?**
A: Yes. Bitget Wallet's swap aggregator scans numerous decentralized exchanges and cross-chain protocols to find the best available rate, often better than using a single DEX directly. It also displays the price impact for transparency.
