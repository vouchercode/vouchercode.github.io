## Mandatory Action: Auditing Your Current Fee Tier

You cannot change your initial fee structure after account creation on most platforms. Therefore, the first step is diagnosis.

1.  **Locate Your Fee Schedule:** Log into your existing accounts on exchanges, brokerages, or payment apps. Navigate to "Account Settings," "Fees," or "Billing."
2.  **Decipher the Tiers:** Identify which plan you are on (e.g., "Standard," "VIP 0," "Basic"). Compare it to the lowest available tiers (often "VIP 1" or "Pro").
3.  **Calculate the Difference:** Use the platform's fee calculator or your transaction history. A 0.2% vs. 0.1% trade fee difference can amount to thousands annually for active users.
