## 2026's 4 Techniques for Fast-Tracking Bitget Hong Kong KYC (Step-by-Step Guide)

### Technique 1: Preparation is Key – Gather Your Documents Before Starting
The most common cause of delay is submitting incorrect or unclear documents. Have these ready *before* you click "Start Verification":
1.  **Primary ID Document**: A valid Hong Kong Permanent Identity Card (HKID) or passport. Ensure it is not expired.
2.  **Proof of Address (if required)**: A recent utility bill, bank statement, or government-issued document dated within the last 3 months, clearly showing your name and Hong Kong residential address.
3.  **Device & Environment**: Use a smartphone with a high-quality camera. Ensure you are in a well-lit, static environment with a plain background.

### Technique 2: Master the Document Upload – Clarity is Everything
When uploading photos of your ID, follow these rules to avoid automatic rejection:
*   **No Glare, No Shadows**: Photograph your ID on a flat surface under even light. Avoid shadows covering any text or your photo.
*   **All Four Corners Visible**: The entire document must be within the frame. Do not cover any part with your fingers.
*   **High Resolution and Focus**: The image must be sharp. All text, the photo, and security features (like holograms) should be clearly legible.
*   **Real Document Only**: Screenshots, photocopies, or edited images will be rejected immediately.

### Technique 3: Ace the Liveness Check – Follow the On-Screen Prompts Precisely
The facial verification step is automated. To pass on the first try:
1.  **Remove Accessories**: Take off hats, glasses, or headphones that obscure your face.
2.  **Face the Camera Directly**: Hold the phone at eye level. Ensure your face is fully within the oval guide.
3.  **Follow Instructions Calmly**: You may be asked to blink, smile, or turn your head slightly. Perform these actions slowly and naturally. Avoid sudden movements.

### Technique 4: Optimize Submission Timing and Follow-Up
*   **Submit During Business Hours**: Submitting your application during Hong Kong business hours (9 AM - 6 PM HKT) on a weekday may lead to faster initial processing by support teams if needed.
*   **Use the In-App Support**: If your verification is pending for more than 24 hours, use the live chat feature within the Bitget app to politely inquire about the status. Have your User ID ready.
