Before starting, have the following ready:
1.  **A Valid Government-Issued Photo ID**: Your Hong Kong Permanent Identity Card (HKID) or a valid passport. Ensure the document is not expired.
2.  **Proof of Address (if required)**: A recent utility bill, bank statement, or government letter issued within the last 3 months, clearly showing your name and Hong Kong residential address.

### Step 4: Submit Personal Information
Fill in the required personal details accurately. This includes:
*   Full name (must match your ID document exactly).
*   ID document number.
*   Date of birth.
*   Residential address in Hong Kong.

### Step 5: Upload ID Document Photos
You will be guided to take photos of your ID document.
*   **Front Side**: Upload a clear, color image of the front of your HKID or passport.
*   **Back Side**: For HKID, upload a clear image of the back side.
*   **Selfie with ID**: You will be asked to take a real-time selfie while holding your ID document next to your face. Ensure both your face and the document details are clearly visible and not blurred.

### Step 6: Facial Recognition (Liveness Check)
The system will prompt you to perform a series of simple actions, such as blinking or turning your head. Follow the on-screen instructions carefully in a well-lit environment. This step confirms you are a real person physically present.

### Step 7: Submit and Wait for Review
After submitting all information and documents, click the final submit button. You will receive an on-screen confirmation and an email notification. The review process typically completes within **15 minutes to 2 hours**, but it can take up to 24-48 hours during peak times.
