## The Testing Methodology: How We Measured Real-World Performance

To ensure objectivity and relevance, our test was designed to mirror the experience of a typical retail investor. We did not rely on advertised "theoretical" processing times from the platforms.

*   **Product Selection**: Identical OKX Dual-Currency Investment products (BTC/USDT pair, similar strike prices and APY) were purchased simultaneously on both Platform A and Platform C using test accounts.
*   **Trigger Point**: The precise moment of product settlement (as recorded on the blockchain and confirmed by both platforms' order histories) was marked as T=0.
*   **Measurement Endpoint**: The endpoint was the moment the funds (both principal and yield) were fully available in the spot wallet and eligible for immediate trading or withdrawal to an external wallet.
*   **Multiple Rounds**: The test was conducted over five separate product cycles to account for network congestion and platform batch processing variability. The results presented are the averages from these cycles.
*   **Environment**: Tests were conducted using standard internet connections, with no preferential API or institutional access.

While speed is crucial, it's part of a broader ecosystem. When choosing a platform for dual-currency products, also consider:
