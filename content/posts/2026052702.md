## Navigating to Binance Futures & Core Concepts

Once your account is funded, navigate to the "**Futures**" section. Before placing a trade, understanding two key strategies is crucial for saving time and managing risk.

### 1. Leverage: Amplifying Your Position
Leverage allows you to control a large contract value with a relatively small amount of capital, known as margin.
* **How it Saves Time**: Instead of waiting to accumulate enough capital for a sizable spot trade, leverage lets you act on market insights immediately.
* **Critical Warning**: While profits are magnified, so are losses. Always use stop-loss orders. Start with low leverage (e.g., 5x-10x) to learn.

### 2. Hedging: Your Risk Management Shield
Hedging involves opening positions to offset potential losses in another. A common method is holding a spot asset (e.g., BTC) while opening a short Futures contract.
* **How it Saves Time**: It protects your portfolio during market downturns without forcing you to constantly monitor and sell your long-term holdings, saving emotional energy and transaction costs.
* **Practical Tip**: Use Futures to hedge your spot portfolio during periods of high volatility or uncertain news events.
