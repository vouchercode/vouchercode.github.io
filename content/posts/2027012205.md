## Common Pitfalls & How to Avoid Them (FAQ)

**Q: I forgot to enter a referral code during registration. Can I add it later?**
A: No. A referral code must be entered during the initial account creation process. It cannot be added, modified, or retroactively applied to an existing account.

**Q: How are my rebate earnings paid out?**
A: Commissions from your referral network are typically credited in real-time or daily to your Binance funding wallet. You can check the detailed breakdown in the [Referral Rewards] -> [History] section.

**Q: Do rebate rules differ for Spot, Futures, and Margin trading?**
A: Yes. The base fee rates and applicable discounts (like the BNB discount) vary per product. Always check the specific fee schedule for Spot, Futures, etc. The referral discount usually applies across major products.

**Q: Is there a minimum trading volume to receive rebates as a referrer?**
A: No minimum volume for your referees is typically required for you to earn commissions. You earn a percentage of whatever trading fees they pay. However, their VIP level (which affects their fee rate) will impact the absolute commission amount you receive.
