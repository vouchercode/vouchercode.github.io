## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: My phone says "Blocked from installing from unknown sources." What do I do?**
**A:** This is a standard Android security feature. Go to your phone's **Settings > Security** (or Apps & Notifications > Special App Access). Find "Install unknown apps" or a similar option, and grant permission to the browser (e.g., Chrome) you used to download the APK file.

**Q: Is the Binance app different from the Binance Lite app?**
**A:** Yes. The main **Binance app** is for all trading features (spot, futures, etc.). **Binance Lite** (or similar simplified apps) is a streamlined version for basic buying/selling. This guide focuses on the full-featured official app.

**Q: The app won't open after installation. What's wrong?**
**A:** First, ensure your phone's operating system is up to date. If you used an APK, it might be corrupted; try downloading it again from the official source. For iOS, ensure your device meets the minimum version requirements.

**Q: How can I verify the app is genuine after installing it?**
**A:** On Android, go to Settings > Apps > Binance > App details. It should show "Binance" as the developer and link to the official store page. On iOS, the developer listed in the App Store should be "Binance."
