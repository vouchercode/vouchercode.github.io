## Why "Permanent High Rebate" is Almost Always a Scam

You've seen the ads: "Sign up with my link for a permanent 80% rebate!" or "Lifetime VIP fee rates!" Let's be brutally honest—these claims are, in most cases, deceptive marketing at best. Here’s the reality check:

*   **The "Permanent" Illusion:** Rebate rates offered by individual partners are **not set in stone by OKX**. They are a share of the partner's own commission. A partner can unilaterally lower your rebate percentage at any time after you sign up, and there's often little recourse.
*   **The "High" Trap:** Extremely high advertised rates (e.g., 70-90%) are frequently short-term promotions or apply only to your first month. The fine print, which no one reads, usually states the rate will drop significantly afterward.
*   **The Hidden Cost:** By chasing an unrealistic "permanent high rebate," you often forfeit other, more stable and valuable benefits offered by official or reputable partner programs, such as welcome bonus tickets, task rewards, and structured tiered benefits.

> **The Bottom Line:** If an offer sounds too good to be true in the crypto space, it almost always is. Your goal shouldn't be the highest possible rebate, but the most **transparent, reliable, and sustainably beneficial** partnership.

## How OKX's Official Partner Rebate System *Actually* Works (2026 Edition)

Understanding the mechanics is your first line of defense. Here’s the breakdown:

1.  **The Fee Flow:** When you trade, you pay a transaction fee (e.g., 0.1%). OKX keeps a portion as its revenue and allocates a portion (typically 20-40% of your fee) as a commission to the partner whose link/code you used.
2.  **Your Rebate:** The partner then shares a percentage of *their commission* back with you. This is your "rebate." If a partner offers a "50% rebate," it means they are giving you 50% of the commission they earned from your trades, **not** 50% of your total trading fee.
3.  **Tiered & Conditional Structures:** Reputable programs have clear, often tiered structures. Your rebate rate might increase based on your 30-day trading volume. Some programs offer bonus rebates for holding OKB or completing specific tasks.

**Q: I already registered without a code. Can I add one later?**
A: No. The invitation code or referral link must be applied **during the initial registration process**. It cannot be added or changed after the account is created.

A: This depends entirely on your partner's policy. Reliable partners process rebates automatically on a daily or weekly basis, usually directly to your OKX funding account or via a supported cryptocurrency. Your dashboard should show pending and processed payments.

**Q: Can OKX itself change my rebate rate?**
