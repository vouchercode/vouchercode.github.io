## Why Should You Immediately Stop Believing the "Lowest Fee" Claim?

*   **The Hidden Spread Trap:** Advertised "trading fees" are only one part of the equation. The bid-ask spread—the difference between the buying and selling price—can be a silent fee eater. A platform might boast a 0.1% fee but have a wider spread than a competitor with a 0.15% fee, making the latter cheaper overall.
*   **Withdrawal Fee Ambush:** Your profit calculation isn't complete until your funds are in your bank account. Some exchanges lure you with low trading fees only to hit you with surprisingly high or opaque fiat withdrawal fees, negating any initial savings.
*   **Liquidity Varies by Pair and Time:** "Lowest fee" claims often refer to major pairs during high liquidity. Selling USDT for a less common fiat currency or during off-peak hours can result in poorer rates and higher effective costs, which the marketing slogan conveniently ignores.
