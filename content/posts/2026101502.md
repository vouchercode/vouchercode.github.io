## Deep Dive: Understanding the 2026 Fee Structure & Transaction Details

Binance's fee structure for card purchases is designed to be transparent. Here’s what you typically see in the breakdown:

2.  **Service/Processing Fee:** This is Binance's charge for the service. As of 2026, it typically ranges from **0.9% to 2.5%**, depending on your region, card type, and payment processor. Higher fees may apply for certain currencies or smaller transactions.
3.  **Total Cost:** (Crypto Amount * Exchange Rate) + Processing Fee.
