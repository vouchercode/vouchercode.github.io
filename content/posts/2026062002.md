## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Are these fees stable, or can exchanges change them suddenly?**
A: Exchanges can and do update their fee schedules. However, changes for major platforms are usually announced in advance. The data here is accurate as of Q1 2026. Always check the official "Fee Schedule" page of your exchange for the most current information.

**Q: Does higher liquidity on Binance justify a slightly higher fee?**
A: Often, yes. Superior liquidity means tighter spreads (the difference between buy and sell prices), which can offset a marginally higher fee by providing better trade execution. For large orders, this is a critical consideration beyond the base fee percentage.

**Q: I'm a very low-volume trader. Which exchange is best for me?**
A: For casual, low-volume traders, **Bitget** might be the most advantageous due to the ease of accessing promotional fee vouchers that can temporarily make your costs near-zero. Binance's BNB discount also helps, but requires upfront purchase of BNB.

**Q: Are there hidden fees like withdrawal costs?**
A: This comparison focuses on trading fees. Always review each platform's deposit and withdrawal fee policies separately, as these can vary significantly for different networks (e.g., ERC-20 vs. BEP-20).
