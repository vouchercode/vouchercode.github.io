## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: I selected "Fast" withdrawal, but it's still pending. What happened?**
A: The "Fast" option sets a high priority fee, but it cannot override a completely saturated blockchain network. Check the TXID on an explorer; if the network is experiencing unprecedented congestion, even high-fee transactions may queue. Your transaction is prioritized but not immune to global network conditions.

**Q: Is it safe to whitelist my withdrawal addresses?**
A: Yes, and it is strongly recommended for security. Whitelisting prevents hackers from withdrawing to new, unauthorized addresses even if they compromise part of your account security. The trade-off is a mandatory waiting period (e.g., 24-48 hours) when adding a *new* address, which is a valuable security cooldown period.

**Q: Can I cancel a withdrawal after submitting it?**
A: Once a withdrawal request is submitted to the blockchain (status changes from "Processing" to "Authorized" or "Broadcasting"), it cannot be canceled by OKX. If it is still in "Processing" due to security review, you may contact support, but cancellations are not guaranteed.
