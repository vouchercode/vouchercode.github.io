### Step 2: Configure the Withdrawal Details
This is the most critical step. You will need to enter:
1.  **Withdrawal Address**: This is the public address of your destination wallet (e.g., another exchange, a hardware wallet, a private wallet). **Triple-check this address.** A single wrong character means lost funds.
2.  **Network**: Choose the correct blockchain network (e.g., TRC20, ERC20, BEP20). **This must match the network supported by your destination address.** Using the wrong network will result in loss. TRC20 is often recommended for lower fees.
3.  **Amount**: Enter the amount of USDT you wish to withdraw. The system will automatically display the network fee.

### Step 3: Security Verification
OKX will prompt you to complete one or more security checks to authorize the transaction. This typically involves:
*   Entering your fund password.
*   Approving the request via your 2FA app (like Google Authenticator).
*   Verifying via an email or SMS code.

### Step 4: Confirm and Submit
Review all details one final time: the amount, the address, the network, and the total deduction (amount + fee). Once confirmed, submit the withdrawal. You will receive a transaction ID (TXID) to track the progress on the blockchain explorer.
