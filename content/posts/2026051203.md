## Feature 5: Advanced Order Types (Beyond Buy & Sell)

Using basic market orders is fine, but advanced orders protect your capital and automate your strategy.

**Must-Know Types:**
*   **Limit Order:** Set the exact price you want to buy or sell at. Your order waits in the order book until the market reaches your price.
*   **Stop-Loss (SL) Order:** **CRITICAL.** Automatically sells an asset if its price falls *to* or *below* a specified level, limiting your potential loss. Always set one for every trade.
*   **Take-Profit (TP) Order:** Automatically sells when the price rises *to* or *above* a target, locking in your profits.

**Screenshot the Order Panel:** Highlight where to select "Limit" and where to input the "Stop-Loss" and "Take-Profit" prices.

## Feature 6: Transparent Fee Schedule & VIP Program

Trading fees eat into profits. OKX uses a maker-taker fee model and offers ways to reduce costs.

**Basics:**
*   **Maker Fee:** Charged when you place an order that adds liquidity to the order book (e.g., a limit order that doesn't fill immediately).
*   **Taker Fee:** Charged when you place an order that removes liquidity (e.g., a market order that fills instantly).
*   **VIP Program:** Your 30-day trading volume and OKB holdings determine your VIP level, which grants progressively lower fees.

**Beginner Tip:** Check the official "Fee Schedule" page. Your current fee rate is always displayed in the trading interface or account settings.

## Common Questions for Beginners (FAQ)

**Q: I deposited funds but don't see them in my Trading Account. Where are they?**
A: Deposits go to your **Funding Account** by default. You must manually transfer them to your **Trading Account** before you can place an order.

**Q: Is setting a Stop-Loss order really that important for a beginner?**
A: Absolutely. It is the single most effective tool to prevent a small loss from becoming a catastrophic one, especially in volatile markets. Make it a non-negotiable habit.

**Q: How long do withdrawals usually take?**
A: Withdrawal processing time varies by blockchain network. After OKX processes the request (which can require email/2FA confirmation), it depends on the network's congestion. ERC-20 (Ethereum) withdrawals, for example, are slower and have higher fees than withdrawals on other networks.

**Q: Are the yields shown in the "Earn" section guaranteed?**
A: No, APYs (Annual Percentage Yields) are projected based on current market conditions and are subject to change. Always read the product details for specific terms and risks.
