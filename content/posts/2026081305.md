## The Step-by-Step Guide to Conquering the "Blurry" OKX KYC in 2026

My breakthrough came from treating the verification not as a quick task, but as a meticulous procedure. Here is the refined process that finally worked.

### Step 1: Preparation is 90% of the Battle
Gather your documents: a **valid passport, national ID card, or driver's license**. Ensure they are physically pristine—no bends, cracks, or worn-out edges that scanners might misread.
*   **Critical:** Use the **latest official OKX App**. Older versions may have compatibility issues with the 2026 verification SDK.
*   **Environment:** Find a room with consistent, bright, overhead lighting. Avoid side lighting that creates shadows. A plain white or light grey wall is the best backdrop.

### Step 2: The Document Scan – Patience Over Speed
1.  Within the OKX App, navigate to **"Account Security" > "Identity Verification."**
2.  Choose your document type. The app will guide you to frame it.
3.  **Do not move.** Let the auto-capture function work. If it struggles, hold the document steady and use the manual shutter button. Ensure all four corners are visible and the text is crystal clear. This is where most "blurry" failures occur.

### Step 3: The Live Selfie – Follow the Prompts Precisely
1.  You will be asked to take a real-time selfie. The AI will give instructions: "Turn head left," "Blink," "Open mouth."
2.  **Perform these actions slowly and deliberately.** Rushing causes motion blur.
3.  Remove hats, glasses, and face coverings. Ensure your face is fully visible and matches the seriousness of your ID photo.

### Step 4: Submission and the Waiting Game
Once submitted, you typically receive a result within **1-2 hours**. If it fails, the app usually specifies why (e.g., "Document edges not clear," "Face match failed"). **Do not immediately resubmit.** Address the specific feedback. If it enters "Manual Review," patience is your only weapon. This can take up to 72 hours.
