## Binance Futures Fee Structure Demystified (2026 Edition)

Binance employs a **maker-taker fee model** for its futures contracts. The key is understanding the difference:

*   **Maker Fee**: You pay this when you place an order that **adds liquidity** to the order book (e.g., a limit order that doesn't fill immediately).
*   **Taker Fee**: You pay this when you place an order that **removes liquidity** from the order book (e.g., a market order or a limit order that fills immediately against an existing order).
