## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Do I need a large amount of money to start a plan like this?**
A: Absolutely not. Alex started with a modest monthly sum. The power is in consistency, not the initial size. Many platforms allow you to start with very small amounts.

**Q: Isn't crypto too volatile for saving something as important as rent money?**
A: The volatility is precisely why a disciplined, long-term DCA strategy is used. It mitigates the risk of investing a lump sum at a peak. The goal isn't to replace your emergency cash fund but to build a separate, growing asset layer over time.

**Q: What if the market crashes right after I start?**
A: This is a feature, not a bug, of the DCA strategy. A crash early in your accumulation phase means you buy more units of the asset for the same money, lowering your overall average cost. The strategy is designed to benefit from market fluctuations over time.

**Q: How do I choose which assets to buy regularly?**
A: Alex focused on large-cap, established assets with long-term track records (like BTC and ETH) for his core DCA plan. He treated more speculative assets separately, with much smaller, discretionary amounts. Research is key.
