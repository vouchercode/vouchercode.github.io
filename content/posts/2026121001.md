1.  Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner and select **[Account]**.
2.  Within the Account settings, look for the option labeled **"Security"** or **"Account Controls."**
3.  Scroll down to find the option for **"Disable Account"** or **"Delete Account."** Binance typically uses "Disable Account" as the first step toward permanent deletion.

### Step 3: Verification and Final Confirmation
1.  You will be asked to complete security verification. This will almost certainly involve your **2FA (Google Authenticator/Authy)** and may include email/SMS verification.

4.  After confirming, your account will enter a **"Disable"** state. **Important:** In many cases, a permanent deletion is processed after a cooling-off period (e.g., 7 days). During this window, you can still log in to reactivate the account. If you do nothing, the account will be permanently erased after this period.
