## Detailed Breakdown of 2026 OKX KYC Methods (Step-by-Step Guide)

OKX typically offers a tiered verification system. The following steps outline the standard process for the most common verification level required for full trading access.

### Method 1: Standard Identity Verification (Most Common & Recommended)

This is the primary method for most individual users, balancing speed and thoroughness.

**Step 1: Access the Verification Section**
Log into your OKX account, navigate to 'Profile' (usually represented by your avatar), and select 'Verification' or 'Identity Verification' from the menu.

**Step 2: Select Your Country/Region and Document Type**
*   Choose your country of residence from the list. **This is critical** as it determines the acceptable forms of ID.
*   Select the type of identity document. OKX commonly accepts:
    *   **International Passport** (Highest acceptance rate globally).
    *   **Government-Issued National ID Card**.
    *   **Driver's License** (in supported regions).

**Step 3: Submit Document Details and Photos**
1.  **Enter Details Manually**: You will be prompted to enter information like your full legal name, document number, and date of birth exactly as it appears on your ID.
2.  **Upload Clear Images**: You will need to upload photos of your document.
    *   **Front Side**: Ensure all details are clear, unobstructed, and the image is well-lit.
    *   **Back Side** (if applicable).
    *   **A "Selfie" or Live Photo**: The system will guide you through taking a real-time photo of your face. Follow the on-screen instructions, ensuring your face is clearly visible and matches the photo on your ID. Good, even lighting is essential here.

**Step 4: Facial Recognition/Liveness Check**
This is a crucial automated step. The system will ask you to perform simple actions like blinking or turning your head to prove you are a real person presenting the document in real-time. This prevents the use of static photos or masks.

**Step 5: Submission and Waiting for Review**
Once submitted, your application enters the review queue. OKX's automated system, complemented by manual checks, processes these verifications.

**Estimated Time:** For straightforward cases with clear document photos, approval can be granted in **as little as 10-30 minutes**. During high traffic periods or if additional checks are needed, it may take up to **24-48 hours**.

### Method 2: Advanced Verification (For Higher Limits or Specific Jurisdictions)

Some users or regions may require additional steps, such as proof of address verification.

*   **Process**: Upload a clear photo or scan of the document in the dedicated section of the verification page.

## Pro Tips to Ensure a Smooth & Secure KYC Process

Follow these guidelines to avoid common pitfalls and speed up your verification:

1.  **Document Quality is Key**: Use a high-resolution camera. Ensure the entire document is in frame, all text is legible, and there is no glare or shadows obscuring details.
2.  **Consistency is Crucial**: Use your full legal name exactly as it appears on your government-issued ID. Avoid nicknames or abbreviations.
3.  **Environment Matters**: For the facial recognition step, find a well-lit, quiet space. Avoid backlighting (e.g., sitting with a window behind you).
4.  **Use the Official OKX App**: The mobile app's camera is often optimized for document capture and liveness checks, potentially making the process smoother than on a desktop browser.
5.  **Beware of Phishing**: Only submit documents through the official OKX website (`www.okx.com`) or the official mobile app. Never send your ID via email or to a third-party claiming to "expedite" your verification.
