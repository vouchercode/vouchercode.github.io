## Bitget KYC Detailed Step-by-Step Guide (2026 Edition)

### Step 1: Access the KYC Portal
Log into your Bitget account. Navigate to **Account Security** or click on your profile icon and find **"Identity Verification"**.
* **Important**: Complete basic account security settings (like 2FA) before starting KYC. Ensure you are using the official Bitget app or website to prevent phishing.

### Step 2: Choose Your Document Type
Select the type of identity document you will use. For Mainland Chinese users, the options are:
1.  **Mainland China ID Card** (Recommended, fastest processing).
2.  **Passport**.
3.  **Driver's License**.

### Step 3: The Photo Submission Process - DO NOT MESS THIS UP
This is the core. You will submit photos for two parts: **Document Photo** and **Facial Verification**.

**Part A: Document Photo (ID Card Example)**
*   **Front Side**: Place your ID card on a dark, non-reflective surface (e.g., a dark table or book). Ensure even, bright lighting with **no shadows, glare, or blur**. All four corners of the ID must be visible within the frame. The text must be crystal clear.
*   **Back Side**: Repeat the process for the back of the ID card.
*   **Live Photo (Handheld)**: You will be asked to take a new photo of yourself holding the ID card next to your face. Follow the on-screen posture guide exactly. Keep a neutral expression, ensure both your face and the ID details are fully visible and in focus. **Do not use a pre-existing photo.**

**Part B: Facial Verification**
*   Follow the on-screen prompts to perform simple actions (like blinking or turning your head).
*   Perform this in a well-lit room. Avoid strong backlighting (like sitting with a window behind you).
*   Maintain a neutral expression and remove glasses or hats if they cause reflection or shadow.

### Step 4: Information Review and Submission
1.  The system will use OCR to auto-fill your name and ID number from the document photos. **Double-check every character for accuracy.** A mismatch leads to rejection.
2.  Manually enter any additional required information, such as your residential address.
3.  Review all information and submitted images one final time before clicking submit.

### Step 5: Wait for Verification
*   **Standard Processing Time**: Typically between **10 minutes to 2 hours**.
*   **Status Updates**: You will receive an in-app and/or email notification upon completion. You can also check the status in the "Identity Verification" section.
*   **If Rejected**: The notification will usually indicate a general reason (e.g., "Document image unclear"). Address the specific issue and resubmit.

## Common Problems & Solutions (FAQ)

**Q: My KYC keeps getting rejected for "document image unclear." What should I do?**
A: This is almost always a lighting/quality issue. Use a second device (like a tablet) as a light source to illuminate the ID evenly without flash. Clean your phone's camera lens. Use the native camera app to take a high-quality photo, then select it from your gallery during the KYC process if the in-app camera is poor.

**Q: Can I use a photocopy or a scanned image of my ID?**
A: **Absolutely not.** The system requires live, real-time photos taken during the process. Uploading pre-scanned images or screenshots will result in failure and may trigger a security lock.

**Q: Is it safe to submit my ID to Bitget?**
A: Bitget uses bank-level encryption (AES-256) to store sensitive data. Information is solely used for regulatory compliance and is not shared with third parties for marketing. Always ensure you are on the official `bitget.com` domain.

**Q: I heard using a VPN can help. Is that true?**
A: For the KYC process itself, using a VPN is **not recommended and may cause issues**. Submit your verification from a stable, local network connection. The requirement is based on your document's nationality, not your IP address at the moment of submission.
