* **Advanced Cloning Techniques:** Scammers use automated tools to create near-perfect replicas of the official OKX website, copying everything from the layout and logos to the footer links and privacy policy text. The only differences are often subtle, like a single misplaced character in the URL.
* **Search Engine Poisoning (SEO Attacks):** Malicious actors invest heavily in black-hat SEO to push their fake sites to the top of search results for terms like "OKX login," "OKX download," or "OKX 官网" (official website).
