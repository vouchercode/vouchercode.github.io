## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: I entered the wrong network for a deposit/withdrawal. What can I do?**
A: Cryptocurrency transactions on a blockchain are irreversible. If funds are sent via an incompatible or incorrect network, they are likely lost permanently. Always triple-check the network before confirming any transaction.

**Q: How long do deposits and withdrawals take?**
A: Crypto deposits/withdrawals depend on blockchain congestion and the required confirmations (can be from 5 minutes to several hours). Fiat deposits via card are usually instant, while bank transfers can take 1-3 business days. Fiat withdrawals to a bank typically take 1-5 business days.

**Q: Why is my withdrawal pending or delayed?**
A: Binance performs security reviews on withdrawals, especially for new accounts, large amounts, or unusual activity. This is for your protection. Ensure all security steps (2FA, email confirmation) are completed. If delayed beyond the estimated time, contact Binance Support.

**Q: Is it safe to leave funds on Binance?**
A: Binance employs robust security measures, including SAFU (Secure Asset Fund for Users). For optimal security, it is recommended to store large amounts of cryptocurrency in a private hardware wallet for long-term holding. Use exchanges like Binance primarily for trading.
