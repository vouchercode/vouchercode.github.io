3.  **Set Alerts**: Configure price alerts 5-10% above and below major identified liquidation clusters. The market often tests these levels.

### Step 2: Interpreting OI Trends for Directional Bias
Use OI to contextualize price action:
*   **Price Up + OI Up**: Strong trend. New longs are entering, supporting continued upward movement. Consider leverage in the direction of the trend, but be wary of excessive OI levels which indicate overcrowding.
*   **Price Down + OI Up**: Bearish signal. New short positions are being opened, suggesting strong selling conviction. **Caution**: This often precedes a "short squeeze" if price reverses.
*   **Price Up + OI Down / Price Down + OI Down**: Trend weakening. Positions are being closed (profit-taking or stop-losses). This often indicates consolidation or an impending reversal. This is a high-risk environment for high leverage.

2.  **Measure the Distance**: Calculate the percentage distance between the current price and the nearest major liquidation cluster.
3.  **The 2026 Adjustment Rule**: If the price is within **15%** of a massive liquidation cluster, **reduce your leverage immediately**.
