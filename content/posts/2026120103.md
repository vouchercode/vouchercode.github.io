## Why Scrutinize Withdrawal Costs? The Silent Profit Killer

You might secure a fantastic trade with low fees, but if a significant chunk is lost upon exit, your net gain diminishes. Withdrawal costs typically consist of two parts:
*   **Exchange Fixed Fee:** The set fee the platform charges for processing the withdrawal.
*   **Network Fee (Gas):** The dynamic cost paid to miners/validators to process the transaction on the blockchain. Exchanges often estimate this and may keep the difference.

The "hidden" cost arises from the lack of transparency in how the network fee portion is calculated and whether the exchange's fixed fee is reasonable. An exchange with low trading fees might compensate with inflated withdrawal charges.

> **Pro-Tip:** Always check the official fee schedule, but remember that listed "network fees" are estimates. The real test is the amount that finally arrives in your wallet.
