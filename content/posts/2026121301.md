## Step-by-Step Guide to Checking and Setting Your OKX Fee Tier (Visual Guide)

### Step 1: Access Your Account Dashboard
Log into your OKX account via the website or mobile app. Navigate to your profile icon, typically in the top-right corner, and select "**Account & Security**" or "**Fee Rate**" from the dropdown menu.

### Step 2: Locate the Fee Tier Section
Within the account settings, find the section labeled "**Fee Rate**," "**Trading Fees**," or "**VIP Level**." This is where your current maker/taker fees and your corresponding tier are displayed.
*   **Important**: If you've never checked this, you are likely on the "Regular User" tier, which is almost never the most economical option.

### Step 3: Verify Your Eligibility for a Better Tier
OKX's fee structure is typically based on two factors: your 30-day trading volume and your OKB holdings. The system should show your current level and the requirements for the next tier.
1.  **Check Trading Volume**: Review your recent trading history to estimate your 30-day volume.
2.  **Check OKB Holdings**: Determine if holding or acquiring a certain amount of OKB could instantly upgrade your tier for greater discounts.

### Step 4: Apply for or Adjust Your Tier
If you qualify for a better tier based on OKB holdings:
1.  Click on "**Upgrade**" or "**Apply**" next to the desired VIP level.
2.  The system will verify your OKB balance and upgrade your tier instantly in most cases.
If your volume qualifies you, the upgrade is usually automatic, but it's critical to monitor this periodically.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: I've been trading for a year on the default fee. Can I get a refund for the overpayment?**
A: Unfortunately, no. Fee tiers are applied prospectively. Exchanges do not retroactively refund fee differences. The key action is to correct your tier immediately to stop the financial bleed.

**Q: Does holding OKB to reduce fees carry any risk?**
A: Yes. While it provides fee benefits, OKB's price is volatile like any other cryptocurrency. You are exposed to market risk on the value of OKB itself. Calculate whether the fee savings outweigh the potential capital risk based on your trading frequency and volume.

**Q: My fee tier was downgraded automatically. Why?**
A: Fee tiers based on trading volume are dynamic. If your 30-day trading volume falls below the required threshold for your current tier, you will be downgraded. Tiers based on OKB holdings are generally stable as long as you maintain the minimum balance.

**Q: Are fee structures the same for spot and futures trading?**
A: No. OKX has separate fee schedules for spot trading, futures, perpetual swaps, and other products. Always check the specific fee page for the product you are using.
