## Why Focus on Official Website Downloads and Deposit Speed?

Before diving into the results, it's crucial to understand our methodology's "why." We focused on the **official website download** for several key reasons:

*   **Security First:** Third-party app stores or unofficial links pose significant security risks, including malware and phishing scams. The only guaranteed safe starting point is the platform's verified official website.
*   **Performance Baseline:** Downloading directly from the source ensures you get the latest, unmodified version of the application, which should offer optimal performance and stability—factors that can indirectly influence transaction processing.
*   **Real-User Simulation:** This test mimics the exact steps a cautious new user would take: find the official site, download the app, register, and make a first deposit. We measured the total time from initiating the download to seeing the funds fully credited and available for trading.

The **deposit speed** was our core performance indicator. In 2026, with advancements in payment rails and blockchain confirmations, users rightfully expect near-instant processing. Delays here translate directly to lost potential profit.
