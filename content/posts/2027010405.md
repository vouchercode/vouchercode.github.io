This is the most straightforward approach if you simply forgot your password.

4.  **Set New Password:** Enter the received code, then create a new, strong password. Use a combination of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.

### Method 2: Regain Access via 2FA Recovery

If you've lost your Google Authenticator or Authy device, use Bitget's dedicated 2FA recovery process.

1.  **Access Recovery Portal:** On the login page, after entering your email/phone, click on "Unable to verify?" or "Lost 2FA?" when prompted for the code.
