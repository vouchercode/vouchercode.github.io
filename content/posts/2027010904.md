My experience was positive, but leverage is a double-edged sword that demands respect. Here is my hard-earned advice:

*   **Never Use Maximum Leverage:** 3x-5x is often more than enough to amplify gains sensibly. 10x, 20x, or higher is a recipe for liquidation on the slightest wiggle. My use of 3x was a deliberate choice to give the trade room to breathe.

*   **Set Orders *Before* Entry:** Your trading plan is worthless if it's not encoded into the platform. Entering a leveraged position without a stop-loss is financial suicide.
*   **Understand Funding Rates:** For perpetual contracts, be aware of funding rates, which can eat into profits or costs on longer-held positions.
